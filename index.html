Thursday 14 MaySiege History
🌟Theme1992–95 Siege
🌞Weather~19°C, mostly clear
🚶Walking~3 km + taxi + cable car
💵Cash needed~60 KM pp
A heavy but essential day. The siege told three ways: personal, underground, and from above.
11:00
🏛️ War Childhood Museum
One of the most powerful museums in Europe. The siege told through 50 objects chosen by people who were children during the conflict — each with a personal testimony. Allow 90 min.
💡 Opens 11am · Book at warchildhood.org
Open in Maps ↗
15:00
🚇 Tunnel of Salvation
The 800m tunnel dug under the airport runway in 1993 that kept the besieged city alive. Museum with original footage and artefacts. Entry ~20 KM.
💡 20-min taxi from centre · Cash only
Open in Maps ↗
17:00
🚡 Trebević — Olympic Bobsled Track
Cable car up to 1,163m for panoramic views, then walk 200m to the abandoned 1984 Olympics bobsled track — covered in murals and graffiti. Eerie and unforgettable.
Open in Maps ↗
Thursday — War Childhood Museum → Tunnel → Trebević

🌿 Optional — Nature

Vrelo Bosne — Stunning natural park 30 min from centre by tram. Crystal-clear springs, swans, horse-drawn carriage along a tree-lined path. Free entry.

Skakavac Waterfall — A 75m cascade north of the city. 9km circular trail, ~3–4 hrs. Free. Wear good shoes.

Food & Drink

Bosnian food is cheap, hearty, and distinctive. You’ll mostly eat čevapi, burek, grilled meats and drink coffee slowly. Most small places are cash only.

Practical Tips
💵
Cash
Always carry KM. Kravice, Tunnel, Počitelj, many museums are cash only. Get KM at an ATM on arrival.
🕌
Mosques
Cover shoulders and knees. Women cover hair. Remove shoes at the entrance. Free outside prayer times.
🚡
Cable Car
Check Trebević operating hours. Bobsled track is 200m from the top station — surreal abandoned history.
🏰️
Počitelj
Wear comfy shoes — steep cobblestone path exposed in May sun. Bring water. Views are worth every step.
💧
Kravice
Entry 20 KM/person. Parking 6 KM/day. Go before 11am to avoid crowds. Swimming May–Sept.
📞
Reservations
Book War Childhood Museum online. WhatsApp for restaurants. Dveri fills fast — cash only.
